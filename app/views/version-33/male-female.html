Which of the following best describes you?


Why are we asking this question?

We use this information to:

  • check whether you already have a National Insurance number
  • and if not, to create a new National Insurance number for you

We understand that not everyone identifies as male or female. We need you to select one of these options even if your identity documents describe you in a different way, if you identify in a diferent way, or if you prefer not to say.

Your answer will not affect your application for a National Insurance number.

We will not give this information to your employer, school or college. Your employer, school or college cannot see this information from your National Insurance number.
