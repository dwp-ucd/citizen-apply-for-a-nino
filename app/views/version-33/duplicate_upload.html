Find out if you can upload your identity documents online

We can only give you a National Insurance number if we have proof of your identity. Depending on which identity documents you have, you may be able to prove your identity online by uploading photos of your documents during a new application for a National Insurance number.



If you have a passport

You can prove your identity online by uploading photos of your passport and any other identity documents as part of your application for a National Insurance number. We will check these documents against UK Home Office or UK Passport Office records.

If you have a British passport

You will need to take and upload:

  • a new photo of your passport
  • a separate photo of yourself holding your passport

If you have an Irish passport

If you only have an Irish passport, we cannot check your identity documents online in either UK Passport Office or UK Home Office records. You will need to attend an appointment to prove your identity after you apply.

If you have a passport from any country other than the UK or Ireland


You will need to take and upload:

  • a new photo of each identity document that you have
  • a separate photo of yourself holding your passport

If you have dual nationality and a British passport

You should use your British passport to apply.



If you do not have a passport

Even if you have other identity documents, you cannot prove your identity online without a passport. You will need to attend an appointment to prove your identity after you apply.
