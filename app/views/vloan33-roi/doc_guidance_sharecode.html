How to apply

You need to generate a new immigration status share code for your application.

✕

A share code:

  • proves your immigration status
  • consists of 9 digits, for example: A1234567Z
  • is different from any reference number you have been given
  • is different from your National Insurance number

Share codes are only valid for 30 days. Generate a new code for this application.

To generate a share code:

  1. Go to www.gov.uk/view-prove-immigration-status - this will open in a new tab.
  2. Follow the instructions to view and prove your immigration status.
  3. When you see what do you need this share code for, select something else.
  4. Write down your share code.
  5. Click Continue to apply for a National Insurance number and enter your share code when asked to do so.
