Do you need a National Insurance number?

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

You only need to apply for a National Insurance number if you:

  • live in the UK and
  • have the right to work in the UK

You must also:

  • be working in the UK or
  • have an offer to start work in the UK or
  • be looking for work in the UK

You do not need to apply for a National Insurance number if you:

  • had a National Insurance number before (read what to do if you have lost your National Insurance number)
  • are a UK resident aged 19 or under
  • have a biometric residence permit (BRP) with a National Insurance number on the back
  • are applying for benefits or a student loan
