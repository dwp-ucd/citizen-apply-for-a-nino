How to apply

1. Fill in the online application


2. Take photos of your passport, of yourself and of your biometric residence permit (BRP)

We will explain the requirements for these photos and show examples of each one.


3. Check and upload photos

We will explain how to check each photo before you upload it.


4. Wait to hear from us

We aim to contact you within 16 weeks to tell you if we have approved your application

We will only use your photos to check your identity. Once we have done this, we will delete them.
