Take and upload a photo of yourself holding your passport next to your face

  1. Get your passport ready

    2. Hold your passport open to the pages that show your picture and your signature.


  2. Take a photo

    3. Using your smartphone, tablet or digital camera, take a photo of yourself holding your passport next to your face.

    In your photo, you must:

    • be facing forwards and looking straight at the camera
    • have a neutral expression and keep your mouth closed
    • have your eyes open and visible
    • not have hair in front of your eyes
    • not have a head covering (unless for religious or medical reasons)
    • not have anything covering your face
    • not have any shadows on your face or behind you
    • not be wearing sunglasses or tinted glasses

    If you need to wear glasses in your photo, make sure your eyes are not covered by the frames or by any glare, reflection or shadow.

    You can ask someone else to take the photograph for you.

    Do not edit your photo. This includes changing the colours, using filters or changing the file type â€“ we can only accept *.JPG, *.JPEG, *.PNG and *.HEIC formats.


  3. Check the photo

    4. Make sure that:

    • your whole face is visible
    • both pages of your passport are visible
    • the photos is in focus

  4. Save the photo

    5. Save the photo to the device you are using to apply for a National Insurance number.
