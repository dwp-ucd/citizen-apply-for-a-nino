Do you have a visa sticker (vignette) in your passport?

If your application for a UK visa was successful, you may have a visa sticker (called a vignette) inside your passport.

The sticker or vignette shows:

  • the type of visa you have
  • the dates your visa is valid
  • the conditions of your visa
