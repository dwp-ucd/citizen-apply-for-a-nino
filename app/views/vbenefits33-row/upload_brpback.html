Take and upload a photo of the back of your biometric residence permit (BRP)

  1. Get your BRP ready

    2. Turn your BRP over so that your picture and the BRP number are facing down


  2. Take a photo

    3. Take a photo using your smartphone, tablet or digital camera, even if this side of your BRP is blank.

    Do not edit your photo. This includes changing the colours or using filters. We can only accept *.JPG, *.JPEG and *.PNG formats.


  3. Check the photo

    4. Make sure that:

    • all four corners of the BRP are visible
    • the photo is in focus
    • we can read any details written on the back of your BRP

  4. Save the photo

    5. Save the photo to the device you are using to apply for a National Insurance number.
