Do you need us to send letters in an alternative format?

If we give you a National Insurance number, we have to send it to you by post. For security reasons, we can't send National Insurance numbers by email.

If you cannot read a standard letter, we can send you an audio version by post, or we can send the letter in Braille or large print.
