How do you need us to send you letters?

If we give you a National Insurance number, we have to send it to you by post. For security reasons, we cannot send National Insurance numbers by email.

We will send you a paper copy of each letter, which you can show to an employer.

We can also send you braille or large print, or an audio version by post.
