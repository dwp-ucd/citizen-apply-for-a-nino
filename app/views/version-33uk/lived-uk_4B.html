When did you first move to the UK?

We need to know when you came to the UK to live or work for the first time. Do not include dates you came here on holiday.

Depending on your immigration status, you may need to give us proof of the date you first moved to the UK.

For example, 31 3 1980



Help with this question

If you have lived or worked in the UK more than once, tell us about the first time you came to live or work here.

If you do not know the exact date you moved to the UK, enter an estimate.
