Help with postcodes
A UK postcode is the last part of your address and is made up of letters and numbers (for example NE98 1YX).
I'd like you to send letters to an address in the Republic of Ireland
If you want us to send letters to an address in the Republic of Ireland and you work in Northern Ireland, you cannot apply for a National Insurance number online.

Call one of the Northern Ireland helpline numbers at the bottom of the screen and we'll tell you how to apply.
