! Warning We cannot make a decision about your application until we receive these photocopies

What you need to do next


  1. 1. Make a note of your application reference number:

    • B0048572

      This is the reference number for your application.
      It is not your National Insurance number.

  2. 2. Make a black and white photocopy of the page of your passport that includes your photograph and your signature.

  3. 3. Make a black and white photocopy of one of the following documents:

      • both sides of your Biometric Residence Permit (BRP) or
      • your most recent UK entry clearance visa

  4. 4. Print the CA5400DN form.

    • Note that your application reference number (B0048572) is entered automatically on page 1 of the form.

  5. 5. Fill in the rest of the form with a black pen using BLOCK CAPITALS.

  6. 6. Put the completed CA5400DN form and your 2 photocopied documents in an envelope.

  7. 7. Add the correct postage and send it to:

    • NINo Allocation
      Mail Opening Unit
      PO Box 42
      Limavady
      BT49 4AN


We aim to contact you within 16 weeks of receiving your photocopied identity documents to tell you if we have approved your application.

We cannot give you an update on your application before the end of this 16-week period.

We are working hard to process applications that have been delayed because of the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic. We will review your application as soon as we can.

You can start work and get paid without a National Insurance number if you can prove you can work in the UK. Your employer might ask for proof of your right to work in the UK.
