Details

You should only fill in this CA5400D form:

  • if DWP told you to do so
  • after you have applied for a National Insurance number

Find out how to apply for a National Insurance number.



Completing this form

You can either print the form and fill it in using a pen or you can:

  1. Save a copy on your computer or laptop.
  2. Open it in Adobe Acrobat Reader version 11 or later.
  3. Fill it in on screen.
  4. Print the completed form.

You also need to send us black and white photocopies of your identity documents and any other documents we have asked you for.

Post this form and your photocopied documents to the address we gave you at the end of your online application for a National Insurance number.



Alternative Formats

If you need information on this service in a different format (such as accessible PDF, large print, easy read, audio recording or braille), contact us using the telephone / textphone number at the bottom of this page.
