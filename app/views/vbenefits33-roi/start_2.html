Before you start

Applying for a National Insurance number is a two-step process.

When you have filled out this online form, there are additional steps you must take to complete your application. We will tell you what you need to do next at the end of this online form.

! Warning If you pause your application for 30 minutes or longer, your session will expire and you will need to start your application again.

If you have any of the following documents, make sure you have them with you before you apply:

  • A passport from any country
  • Biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • National identity card

You may need to enter information from these documents during your application â€“ for example, your passport number.

If you do not have any of these documents, we may ask you for other information.
