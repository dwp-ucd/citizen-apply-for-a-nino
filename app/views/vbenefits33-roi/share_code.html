! WarningOnly fill in this form if the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) sent you a text message or a letter asking you to do so.

As part of your application for a National Insurance number, you gave us an immigration status share code.

The share code you gave us has expired or is invalid. You need to generate a new share code.

A share code:

    • consists of 9 digits (letters and numbers) – for example: AB123456Z
    • is different from your 16-digit EU Settlement Scheme application reference number
    • is different from your National Insurance number
    • will only work for your application – you cannot pass your share code on to someone else, or use someone else’s code

Share codes are only valid for 30 days. Generate a new share code and enter it below straight away.

To generate a share code:

    1. Go to www.gov.uk/view-prove-immigration-status - this will open in a new tab
    2. Follow the instructions to view and prove your immigration status
    3. When you see what do you need this share code for, select something else
