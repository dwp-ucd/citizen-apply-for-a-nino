What is a biometric residence permit (BRP)?

A BRP is a secure identity card which contains personal details and biometric information.

You will usually get a BRP if you:

  • apply to come to the UK for more than 6 months
  • extend your visa to more than 6 months
  • apply to settle in the UK
  • transfer your visa to a new passport
  • apply for certain Home Office travel documents

A BRP looks similar to a UK residence card, but they are different.

You may already have a National Insurance number on the back of your BRP. If so, you can start using this National Insurance number now, and close this application.
