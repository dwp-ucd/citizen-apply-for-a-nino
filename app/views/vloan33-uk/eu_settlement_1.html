Have you applied to the EU Settlement Scheme?

You do not need to have settled or pre-settled status to get a National Insurance number.

What is the EU Settlement Scheme?

If you are from the EU, Switzerland, Norway, Iceland or Lichtenstein, the EU Settlement Scheme allows you and your family to continue living in the UK.

The deadline for most people to apply was 30 June 2021, but you may still be able to apply.
