Has your parent or guardian ever claimed Child Benefit for you?

Most parents and guardians who are responsible for bringing up a child / children will claim Child Benefit.


Why are we asking this question?

If your parent or guardian claimed Child Benefit for you, it is likely that we:

  • created a National Insurance number for you
  • sent you a letter about it in the 3 months before your 16th birthday

We might have sent the letter to your previous address.
