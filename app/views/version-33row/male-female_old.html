Which of the following best describes you?

Use the sex recorded on your birth certificate, on your Gender Recognition Certificate, or that you feel best describes you.



Why are we asking this question?

We only use this information to create a new National Insurance record in our database.

We understand that not everyone identifies as male or female. Due to technical constraints, we are currently only able to record these two options. We need you to select one of these even if your identity documents describe you in a different way, if you identify in a different way, or if you prefer not to say.

Your answer will not affect your application for a National Insurance number.

We will not give this information to your employer, school or college. Your employer, school or college cannot see this information from your National Insurance number.
