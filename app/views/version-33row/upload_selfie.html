Take and upload a photo of yourself holding your passport next to your face

We will manually check the image of your face to make sure it matches the photo in your passport.


  1. Get your passport ready

    2. Hold your passport open to the pages that include your picture.


  2. Take a photo

    3. Using a smartphone, tablet or digital camera, take a photo of yourself holding your passport next to your face. Someone else can take the photo for you.

    Your whole face must be clearly visible, and we need to be able to see that the passport photo is of you. You must:

    • be facing forwards with a neutral expression and your eyes open
    • not have anything covering your face
    • not have any dark shadows hiding your face
    • not be wearing sunglasses or tinted glasses

    If you need to wear glasses in your photo, make sure your eyes are not covered by the frames or by any glare, reflection or shadow.


    ! WarningDo not edit your photo.
    This includes changing the colours and using filters. We can only accept *.JPG, *.JPEG and *.PNG formats.



  3. Check the photo

    4. Make sure that:

    • your whole face is visible
    • all four corners of your passport are visible
    • the photo is in focus

  4. Save the photo

    5. Save the photo to the device you are using to apply for a National Insurance number.
