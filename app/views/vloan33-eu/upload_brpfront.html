Take and upload a photo of your biometric residence permit (BRP)

  1. Get your BRP ready

    2. Put your BRP on a flat surface so that your picture and the BRP number are visible.


  2. Take a photo

    3. Take a photo using a smartphone, tablet or digital camera. You can get someone else to take the photo for you.

    To avoid light reflection, take the photo in a bright room and turn the flash off.

    Make sure that:

    • all four corners of the BRP are visible
    • the photo is in focus
    • the details on your BRP are clear enough to read

    ! WarningDo not edit your photo.
    This includes changing the colours and using filters. We can only accept *.JPG, *.JPEG and *.PNG formats.



  3. Check the photo

    4. Make sure that the photo meets the requirements listed above.


  4. Save the photo

    5. Save the photo to the device you are using to apply for a National Insurance number.
