Take and upload a photo of your passport

  1. Get your passport ready

    2. Hold your passport open to the pages that include your picture.


  2. Take a photo

    3. Take a photo of both pages using a smartphone, tablet or digital camera. You can get someone else to take the photo for you.

    Make sure that:

    • all four corners are visible
    • the photo is in focus
    • the details on your passport are clear enough to read

    ! WarningDo not edit your photo.
    This includes changing the colours and using filters. We can only accept *.JPG, *.JPEG and *.PNG formats.



  3. Check the photo

    4. Make sure that the photo meets the requirements listed above.


  4. Save the photo

    5. Save the photo to the device you are using to apply for a National Insurance number.



Help with taking photos

If you are struggling to keep your passport open to the relevant pages, you can hold it open. Your hand can be visible in the photo, provided it does not cover any of the text or images.

To avoid light reflection, take the photo in a bright room and turn the flash off.
